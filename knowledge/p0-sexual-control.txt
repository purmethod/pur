═══ THE PELVIC FLOOR ═══
The pelvic floor is the most important muscle group in male sexual function. It controls erection quality, ejaculatory control, bladder function, and sexual stamina. Most men have never deliberately trained it. Most men don’t even know it exists as a trainable muscle.

The pelvic floor consists of the pubococcygeus (PC) muscle and surrounding muscles that form the base of the pelvis. In men, this muscle group directly controls:

- The rigidity of erections (blood retention in the corpus cavernosum)
- The timing and force of ejaculation
- The ability to separate orgasm from ejaculation
- Urinary control and prostate health

Science: Dorey et al., 2005 (BJU International) — Pelvic floor muscle training resolved erectile dysfunction in 40% of participants after 3 months, with significant improvement in a further 35%. Results comparable to pharmaceutical intervention (Viagra) without side effects.

═══ PORNOGRAPHY — THE DOPAMINE POISON ═══

The solution is not willpower. The solution is understanding. Once you understand that pornography is literally rewiring your brain against your own body, the decision to stop becomes obvious.
